Time vs Efficiency

The Pareto frontier: algorithms in the top-left corner are the best tradeoff between speed and packing quality.

Recommendation: Use OBFD as the default — it's near-optimal in efficiency with sub-millisecond latency. Switch to NF or HK only if you need streaming, as these are the only two streaming-capable algorithms in SeqPacker.
